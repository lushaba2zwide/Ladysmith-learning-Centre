Our Mission

Ladysmith Learning Centre was founded in 2020 by Mr S. Zwane with a clear vision: to give every person in Ladysmith access to quality education, regardless of their age or background.

What started as a small tutoring initiative has grown into a trusted learning centre serving hundreds of learners — from Grade 8 pupils needing after-school support, to adults upgrading their matric and anyone in the community who simply wants to learn and improve their life.

Our tutors are passionate, experienced, and aligned with the South African CAPS curriculum. We create a welcoming, judgement-free space where every learner is encouraged and supported.

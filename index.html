How to Enrol

Getting started is simple. Follow these four steps and you'll be in class in no time.

01

Contact Us

Call us on 072 983 7173 or send an email to ladysmithlearningc@gmail.com to express your interest.

02

Choose Your Subjects

Tell us which subjects you need help with. We'll advise you on the best programme — after-school tutoring, one-on-one, or matric upgrade.

03

Pay Registration

Pay the once-off R500 registration fee via Capitec Bank (Acc: 1591378913) to secure your place.

04

Start Learning

Attend your first class at 160 Murchison Street, Old Riga Building, Room 3. Classes run Mon–Fri 08:00–15:00.
